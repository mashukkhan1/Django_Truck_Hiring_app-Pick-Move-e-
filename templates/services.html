Pick & Move : Services

Pick & Move is the largest online platform in Bangladesh to hire pickup, truck or covered van in minutes. It assists you to find trucks for your trips. Predominantly it aims to make truck booking more accessible to everyone for their business or personal needs. Vehicles of all sizes and shapes are available here for hire. Pick & Move is fast, affordable, secured. It is free to use for shippers. It ensure lowest price to hire a service. Multiple truck hiring is available too. There's no middle man. Customers can hire truck directly through Pick & Move. All vehicles are verified. Hiring trucks for any pick-up point and drop off point in Bangladesh is possible. All sizes and shapes of trucks are available here.

We offer different categories of truck according to your needs:


  • 1 Ton Truck

    In the market, 1 ton category trucks are the smallest in size. Shippers need these trucks to meet their minimum requirements. These trucks are used mainly for the transport of small business products. Several brands of 1 ton category trucks are there in the market. Among them, Tata, Mahindra, Toyota etc are the major brands.



  • 2 Ton Truck

    Customers usually pick a truck considering the amount of goods he or she needs to get transported. The 2-ton category trucks are slightly larger than 1-ton category. These trucks can be used for transporting minor goods or business products. There are several popular brands on the 2-ton category like Tata, Isuzu, Toyota etc.



  • 3.5 Ton Truck

    3.5-ton category trucks are one of the most widely used ones. It can be used for transportation of business goods or for house shifting if there are a lot of items. Therefore, we can call it an ’All-rounder’ truck. Among the popular brands of this category Eicher, Mahindra, Tata, Mitsubishi, JMC etc. are noteworthy.



  • 7.5 Ton Truck

    To carry out industrial goods, big traders and businessmen prefer trucks of 7.5-ton. Basically, the fleet of heavy trucks starts from this category. There are a number of brands in this category. These are Tata, Isuzu etc.



  • 15 Ton Truck

    15 ton trucks are mostly used by big companies or industries. These trucks are very popular for transportation of industrial heavy goods and business products. Eicher, Tata, Ashok Leyland etc. are among the popular brands in this category.



  • 25 Ton Truck

    25 ton trucks are used for the transportation of heavy industrial goods. These trucks are distinct for their use of 10 tires. Among the popular brands Tata, Ashok Leyland are notable.



  • Prime Mover/Trailer/Lorry

    The longest vehicle carrying four axles or fourteen-wheel containers is what we call Prime Movers. These are also known as trailers or lorry. Prime mover’s are mostly used in export-import industry. DAF, Eicher, Tata etc are among the major brands of prime movers.
