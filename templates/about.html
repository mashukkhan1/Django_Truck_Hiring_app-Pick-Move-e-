Pick & Move : Hire Truck With Ease

Pick & Move is the largest online platform in Bangladesh to hire pickup, truck or covered van in minutes. It assists you to find trucks for your trips. Predominantly it aims to make truck booking more accessible to everyone for their business or personal needs. Vehicles of all sizes and shapes are available here for hire. Pick & Move is fast, affordable, secured. It is free to use for shippers. It ensure lowest price to hire a service. Multiple truck hiring is available too. There's no middle man. Customers can hire truck directly through Pick & Move. All vehicles are verified. Hiring trucks for any pick-up point and drop off point in Bangladesh is possible. All sizes and shapes of trucks are available here. Common deliveries our shippers make:

  • FMCG Products
  • Livestock & Poultry Products
  • Furniture industry
  • Construction materials
  • Oversized goods
  • Garments materials
  • Export & import materials
  • Agricultural items
  • Home shift
Here’s how it works: First, select if you need pickup/truck instantly or later

For instant need-

  • Set delivery routes
  • Select your vehicle type(1/2ton)

  • Mention the date & time for pick-ups/trucks

  • Get the confirmation notification

Guess what? You have just hired a truck. It’s that easy.
